This Privacy Policy explains how Baseflow collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you visit docs.baseflow.biz, use Baseflow documentation, or communicate with Baseflow about its products and services.

Baseflow documentation is intended for business and technical users. It is not directed to children under 13.

How We Use Information

Baseflow uses information to:

  • Provide, operate, secure, and improve Baseflow documentation, products, and services.
  • Respond to questions, support requests, feedback, and business inquiries.
  • Monitor site performance, diagnose issues, and prevent abuse or unauthorized access.

Cookies and Local Storage

The documentation site may use cookies, browser local storage, or similar technologies to support basic site functionality, remember preferences, improve search, and collect aggregate usage or security information. You can manage cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect site functionality.

How We Share Information

Baseflow does not sell personal information. Baseflow may also disclose information when required by law, to protect rights and safety, or in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or transfer of business assets.

Data Retention

Baseflow keeps information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide documentation, products, services, support, security, business records, and legal compliance. Retention periods may vary based on the type of information and the reason it was collected.

Security

Baseflow uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so Baseflow cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction, or objection regarding personal information. You may also have the right to opt out of certain processing or lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

International Transfers

Baseflow may process information in the United States and other locations where Baseflow or its service providers operate. Those locations may have data protection laws different from the laws where you live.

Changes to This Policy

Baseflow may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When updates are made, Baseflow will revise the "Last updated" date above.

Contact
